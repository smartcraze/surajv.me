Slotify is a production-grade scheduling and booking platform built to deliver the smooth, low-friction experience users expect from modern tools like Cal.com, while keeping the stack lean, type-safe, and developer-friendly.

It is not just a booking page. It is a complete end-to-end scheduling system where hosts can easily share their availability and guests can discover accurate time slots in their local timezone.

## What It Does

Most scheduling products solve only one part of the workflow. With Slotify, the goal was to build a cohesive engine connecting all parts reliably:

- **Timezone-Aware Scheduling**: Computes dynamic availability across regions. Host availability is handled in their timezone, attendees view options in their local timezone, and all data is securely persisted in UTC.
- **Calendar & Meeting Integration**: Integrates directly with Google Calendar to reflect booked sessions and automatically generate Google Meet join links.
- **Robust Booking Lifecycle**: Manages creations, cancellations, and reschedules with conflict-safe concurrency handling to prevent double bookings under load.
- **Payments & Subscriptions**: Integrates Razorpay for premium features, utilizing secure webhook processing for payment lifecycle events.
- **Reliable Notifications**: Employs Resend for transactional emails, with delivery-status synchronization to verify users are actually receiving their updates.

## Technical Stack

Slotify leverages a modern, high-performance web stack:

- **Framework**: Next.js 16 (App Router) + React 19 + TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4 + Radix/shadcn UI
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Authentication**: Better Auth with Google OAuth
- **Integrations**: Razorpay (Payments) & Resend (Emails)
- **Tooling**: Bun runtime
